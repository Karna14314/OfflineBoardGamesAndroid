Privacy Policy for Offline Board Game Hub

Last updated: March 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Thank you for playing Offline Board Game Hub. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data collection and usage within our mobile application.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Offline Board Game Hub is designed to be played entirely offline. However, to support the development of the app, we utilize a third-party advertising service (Google AdMob).

Local Data

All game progress, settings, and save files are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit, collect, or store this data on external servers.

Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain device and usage information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location data (at a non-precise level, e.g., city or country)
  • App interaction data metrics related to served ads

For more information on how Google uses data, please review Google's Privacy & Terms.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party services or use third-party libraries (such as Google Play Services and AdMob). These services have their own privacy policies. We strongly recommend reviewing them:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Policies

4. Children's Privacy

Our application is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us (details below), and we will take necessary steps to remove that information from the third-party services we utilize.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us by leaving feedback or opening an issue on the app's store page or source repository.
